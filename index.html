About the CEO

Mr. Anish Mahajan

Born on 22 October 2003 into a middle-class family, Mr. Anish Mahajan exhibited entrepreneurial ambition from an early age. Although his family previously operated a school business that closed in 2010, Anish envisioned a new future.

In 2018, while surrounded by family members working as government officials, he made a bold decision to pursue business — diverging from the family trend. Unlike his siblings and cousins who chose science streams, Anish pursued commerce in 11th and 12th grades despite family resistance, and excelled in his academics.

With a keen interest in real estate, he considered various career paths after school. While his family urged him to pursue engineering, Anish stayed true to his vision and chose BBA in Business Analytics — becoming the first in the family to do so. Now in his third year, his journey is a testament to determination, vision, and resilience.

Mr. Mahajan stands as a modern entrepreneur embracing tradition, innovation, and the spirit of independent thinking — values that guide The Anish Mahajan Co. every day.
